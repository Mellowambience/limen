# ADR-0004: Hyperspace, Subspace, and J-Space

- **Status:** accepted
- **Date:** 2026-07-10

## Context

LIMEN needs language and mechanisms for broad possibility search, subconscious-lite incubation, and cross-device travel. These ideas must remain technically honest and must not become permissionless propagation.

## Decision

Implement three inspectable local systems:

1. **Hyperspace** for parallel branch generation and safe recommendation.
2. **Subspace** for private incubation and dream-like pattern surfacing without external action.
3. **J-Space (Junction Space)** for explicitly paired hosts, journey plans, and return anchors.

All three are computational metaphors. External actions remain governed by the Constitution and permission envelopes.

## Consequences

- LIMEN can use mythic language without making unsupported physics claims.
- Travel remains possible across authorized machines through existing Wingseed Capsules.
- No ambient device discovery or self-propagation is permitted.
- Private Subspace material remains local unless separately approved for export.
